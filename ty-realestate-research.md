## 1. Prescott, AZ Real Estate Market Overview

### Market Fundamentals
- **Median Home Price:** $581K-$626K (January 2026)
- **Price per Square Foot:** $301-$332
- **Market Position:** 36% higher than national average
- **Days on Market:** 78 days average (down from 104 days YoY)
- **Market Competition:** Somewhat competitive (Redfin score: 45/100)

### Market Trends
- **Year-over-Year Change:** Down 1.5% in January 2026 (following previous 14.11% growth)
- **Sale-to-List Ratio:** 97.2% (+0.9 pt YoY)
- **Market Stability:** Steady growth with moderate inventory increase
- **Price Range Growth:** Significant increases across all segments over past 5 years

### Demographics & Buyer Profiles

**Primary Buyer Segments:**
1. **Retirees** - Largest segment, seeking active adult communities
2. **Remote Workers** - Post-2020 influx from urban areas
3. **Young Professionals** - Growing segment per 2025 data
4. **Second Home Buyers** - Attracted to lifestyle and climate

**Migration Patterns:**
- **Inbound from:** Phoenix (1,183), Los Angeles (800), Chicago (423), Seattle (364)
- **Outbound to:** Destin FL, Knoxville TN, St. George UT
- **53%** of buyers stay within Prescott metro area

**Market Drivers:**
- Small-town charm with modern amenities
- Mild climate and outdoor recreation
- Affordable housing compared to larger metros
- Appeal to remote workers seeking quality of life

## 6. Website Best Practices & Technical Requirements

### Technical Foundation
- **Hosting:** Fast, reliable hosting (3-second load time max)
- **SSL Certificate:** Security for lead forms
- **Mobile-First Design:** Responsive across all devices
- **Schema Markup:** Real estate-specific structured data
- **Analytics Setup:** Google Analytics 4, Tag Manager

### IDX Integration Requirements
- **MLS Data Feed** - Real-time listing updates
- **Advanced Search** - Price, beds/baths, location filters
- **Map Integration** - Interactive property mapping
- **Saved Searches** - User account functionality
- **Lead Capture** - Registration gates for detailed info

### Lead Capture Optimization
**Primary Forms:**
1. **Property Inquiry** - Listing-specific questions
2. **Home Valuation** - Seller lead generation
3. **Buyer Consultation** - Qualification and preferences
4. **Newsletter Signup** - Market updates and insights
5. **Showing Request** - Immediate scheduling

**Conversion Optimization:**
- Progressive profiling (minimal initial fields)
- Social proof near forms
- Clear value proposition
- Mobile-optimized forms
- Thank you page with next steps

### Content Management
- **Blog Platform** - WordPress or similar CMS
