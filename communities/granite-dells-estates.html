Welcome to Granite Dells Estates

Nestled within the heart of Sunset Ridge in the 1,100-acre master-planned Granite Dells development, this exclusive gated community offers refined living surrounded by dramatic rock formations and breathtaking views.

Each home features spacious open layouts with 10' ceilings and 8' solid core interior doors throughout. The gourmet kitchens include roomy islands and walk-in pantries perfect for entertaining. Luxurious primary suites boast large walk-in closets, double executive-height sinks, walk-in showers, and deep soaking tubs.

Customize your home at the Design Center to match your unique style. Select homes offer RV garage options for your recreational vehicles.
