Discover Hidden Hills

Hidden Hills offers a private setting with breathtaking views of the Prescott National Forest. This intimate community features homes on oversized 0.25-acre lots, giving you plenty of space and privacy.

The peaceful atmosphere and natural surroundings make Hidden Hills ideal for those seeking a quiet retreat while still being close to all Prescott amenities. Wake up to forest views and enjoy the serenity of mountain living.

Each home is designed with Capstone's signature quality and attention to detail, featuring open floor plans, gourmet kitchens, and luxurious primary suites.
