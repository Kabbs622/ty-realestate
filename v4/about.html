The Person Behind the Website

I'm Ty. Let's Get to Know Each Other.

Six years ago, I packed up my life in Utah and drove to Prescott with nothing but a U-Haul and a feeling that this was the right move. I didn't know a soul. I didn't have a job lined up. I just knew I needed to be here.

Best decision I ever made.

Why I Do What I Do

When I first moved here, I worked with an agent who treated me like a transaction. You know the type – shows you three houses, pressures you to pick one, then disappears once the contract is signed. I swore I'd never do that to someone else.

So I became an agent. But not just any agent. I went in-house with Capstone and Ecco because I believe in what they're building. I know these communities inside and out because I spend my days in them. I've walked every model home. I know which lots get morning sun and which ones have the best views.

Here's what you should know about me: I respond to texts at 9 PM because I know you're thinking about floor plans after the kids go to bed. I'll tell you if a community isn't right for you, even if it means less commission. And I'll be there at your closing, not because I have to be, but because I want to see you get those keys.

When I'm not selling homes, you'll find me hiking Granite Mountain, playing pickleball at the J Club, or exploring the trails with my dog. Prescott isn't just where I work – it's where I live, play, and plan to stay.

What Working With Me Looks Like

1. We Talk

First, I listen. What do you need? What's your timeline? What's your budget reality? I'll be honest about what works and what doesn't.

2. We Tour

I'll show you the communities that fit. We'll walk models, check out lots, and I'll tell you the stuff the salespeople won't – like which way the afternoon sun hits or where the HOA drama happens.

3. We Negotiate

As an in-house agent, I have relationships with these builders. I know where there's wiggle room and where there isn't. You won't pay more than you should.

4. I Stay

My job doesn't end at closing. I'll check in during construction, help you navigate the process, and be there when you get your keys. Then I'll be there for the housewarming party.
